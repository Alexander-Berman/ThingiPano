About

ThingiPano contains metadata, images, and panoramic depth-map renderings of 3D designs from Thingiverse up through 2008. Examples applications of the dataset are highlighted in the paper "ThingiPano: A Large-Scale Dataset of 3D Printing Metadata, Images, and Panoramic Renderings for Exploring Design Reuse" to be published in IEEE MultiMedia Big Data (IEEE BigMM) 2020.
